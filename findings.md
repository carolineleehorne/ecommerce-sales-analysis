# Key Findings

Analysis of 60 orders across Jan–Jun 2023.

## Revenue
- Total revenue: $18,847.20 across 60 completed orders
- Average order value: $314.12
- Electronics drove 68% of total revenue despite being one of three categories

## Regional Performance
- West region led in total revenue ($6,240.80)
- East had the highest order volume (22 orders)
- South had the lowest average order value — potential opportunity for upsell

## Products
- Monitor 27in was the highest revenue product ($3,990.00 total)
- Wireless Mouse had the most units sold across the period
- Standing Desk had strong revenue despite lower order count due to high unit price

## Customer Behavior
- 70% of customers placed repeat orders within the 6-month window
- High-value customers (5+ orders) averaged $847 lifetime value
- C201 and C312 were the most active customers by order count

## Trends
- Revenue grew 18% from Q1 to Q2
- Largest single-month jump: March to April (+22%)
- Average order value remained stable — growth driven by volume, not price increases

## Customer Segmentation Detail
- Segmented customers into three tiers: High Value (5+ orders), Mid Value (3-4), Low Value (1-2)
- High Value segment had an average lifetime value 3.2x greater than Low Value
- Repeat customer rate of 70% is strong — suggests product-market fit and healthy retention
- Opportunity: Low Value customers who ordered Electronics may respond to targeted re-engagement
